YOUR LIFE AT HOME


The Challenge:
To design an SEO project for Hillarys Blinds Ltd which shows you how many hours of your life you've spent doing various things in your home.

What I did:
I designed this site with the intention on it being heavily illustrative as previous SEO projects of this style had worked sucsessfully. As the site is data heavy, illustrations were the best solution to help the user digest the data easily and my research showed this. I therefore continued to develop my designs into simple, bite sized and categorised sections in order to help users to visualise the scale of what they have achieved. I added the idea of subtle illustrations to keep the user more immersed within the tool, yet not too many so that they became distracted.
The Outcome:
The site was picked up by a variety of journalists from well known publications who all commented on how keen they were. Some major newspapers had also passed it around their teams and commented that they 'loved it'.
