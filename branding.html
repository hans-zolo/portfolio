BRANDING
LINCEX

The Brief: To create a logo and brand for Lincex Consultants.

The client wanted a corporate feel to the brand, with an image of the Lincoln cathedral being a key element to ensure that the brand reflects its local roots.

THE FEDERATION OF CORPUS CHRISTI

The Brief: To create a brand and logo for The Federation of Corpus Christi.

Corpus Christi means 'the body of Christ' and this is where the idea for the circle element of the logo came from. It resembles the breaking of the bread, while the cross represents Christ and the support and stability that the federation gives their pupils.
NRPS


The Brief: To create a brand and logo for Northfield Road Primary School focused around the image of an anchor as the school is based in the town where the anchor for the Titanic was made.

Anchor- a person or thing which provides stability or confidence in an otherwise uncertain situation.

The client loved my rough sketches and the texture that I'd created, and so I continued to develop a rustic 'sketch style' logo.
